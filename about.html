Kory Ricketts

Founder & Program Director, Laissez Fair Digital Studio

Kory Ricketts is the founder of Laissez Fair Digital Studio, a community-focused technology education initiative dedicated to expanding access to digital literacy, STEAM learning, and workforce readiness programs.

He currently serves as STEAM Studio Coordinator and Technology Manager at Spotlight Scholars Microschool, where he leads project-based learning experiences for elementary students and supports school-wide technology integration. He also works as an IT Support Technician at Liberty University and holds a CompTIA IT Fundamentals (ITF+) certification.

In addition, Kory serves as the Head Instructor and Director of Technology Programs for Shevet Preparatory Academy, where he leads the development and implementation of technology and systems design programs for middle and high school students.

Kory specializes in designing scalable, project-based learning systems that bridge foundational digital skills with real-world application. Through Laissez Fair Digital Studio, he partners with schools, libraries, community organizations, and workforce programs to deliver structured learning pathways—from K–12 STEAM education using platforms like CoSpaces EDU (Delightex) to college, career, and workforce readiness programs aligned with emerging technologies and CTE pathways.

His work is centered on helping learners build digital confidence, creativity, and practical technology skills needed to succeed in today’s evolving digital landscape.
